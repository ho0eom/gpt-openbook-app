This book is about exploratory data analysis, about looking 
at data to see what it seems to say. It concentrates on 
simple arithmetic and easy-to-draw pictures. It regards 
whatever appearances we have recognized as partial 
descriptions, and tries to look beneath them for new insights. 
Its concern is with appearance, not with confirmation.
